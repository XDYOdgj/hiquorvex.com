Experience Enhanced Bathroom Therapy with KOHLER G90

The KOHLER G90 is a smart, multifunctional showerhead that brings convenience and comfort to your daily routine. With advanced temperature and rainfall sensors, it automatically adjusts to your preferences for a personalized and relaxing shower experience. Whether you're taking a bath, shower, or using the hand shower mode, the G90 ensures consistent water flow and temperature with its intuitive touch controls. The sleek brushed nickel finish adds a touch of elegance to any bathroom decor, while the durable design makes it suitable for use with garden hoses and travel setups. Featuring 2.5 GPM water flow and a maximum pressure rating of 80 psi, this smart shower is built to last and deliver performance without compromise.
